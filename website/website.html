THESE ARE THE BEST FIVE WEBSITES TO VISIT ON THE INTERNET IN 2024!

FACEBOOK
History: Facebook was founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and roommates. It expanded globally and became a social media giant.
Fun Fact: Facebook helps you connect with friends and family. It allows you to join groups and communities.
GOOGLE
History: Google was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. It began as a search engine and expanded its services greatly.
Fun Fact: Google makes finding information online simple and quick. It's essential for daily chores, recreation, and employment.
INSTAGRAM
History: Instagram launched in 2010 by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger. Facebook acquired it in 2012, expanding its features.
Fun Fact: Instagram lets you share photos and videos creatively. Follow friends, influencers, and explore new trends.
YOUTUBE
History: YouTube was created in 2005 by three founders. Google acquired it in 2006, recognizing its potential.
Fun Fact: YouTube offers endless entertainment with diverse video content. It's great for learning and following favorite creators.
TIKTOK
History: TikTok was launched globally in 2016 by ByteDance. It merged with Musical.ly in 2018, enhancing its popularity.
Fun Fact: TikTok lets you create and watch engaging short videos. It's great for expressing creativity and discovering trends.
